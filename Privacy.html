Privacy Policy

Bank Bonus Tracker  ·  Last updated: March 2026

What this app is

Bank Bonus Tracker is a personal finance tool that helps individuals track bank account opening bonuses, requirements, deadlines, and payout dates. It is a free, open-source application with no backend server. All data processing happens entirely in your browser.

What data we collect

We do not collect any data. There is no server, no database, and no analytics. The app stores your bonus tracking data in two places — both under your sole control:

  • Your browser's localStorage — data stays on your device only.
  • Your personal Google Drive — only if you choose to connect Drive. The file is stored in your own Google account and is never accessible to anyone else.

Google Drive access

If you choose to connect Google Drive, this app requests the following permissions:

  • drive.file — allows the app to create and update a single JSON file (bank-bonus-tracker.json) in a folder called Bank-Bonus-App-Data in your Google Drive. The app can only access files it creates — it cannot read, modify, or delete any other files in your Drive.

We never transmit, share, sell, or access your Google Drive data for any purpose other than saving and loading your bonus tracking data at your explicit request.

Third-party services

The app uses the Google Drive API solely to provide optional cloud backup. Your use of Google Drive is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services are used.

Data retention and deletion

You are in complete control of your data at all times:

  • Clear browser localStorage at any time through your browser settings.
  • Delete your Drive file directly from Google Drive at any time.
  • Revoking the app's Google Drive access via Google Account Permissions immediately removes all access.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact
